Privacy Policy

Fidget Strings

Last updated: November 2025

Fidget Strings does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics services

Data Storage

All app preferences (such as sound and haptic settings) are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Network Usage

Fidget Strings does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline and does not communicate with any servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
